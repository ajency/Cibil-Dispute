Welcome to CIBIL dispute center
What should I do if I find inaccuracies in my CIBIL report?

You can now initiate a dispute online with CIBIL to address inaccuracies, account ownership and duplication of information appearing in your credit report. CIBIL will assist you in reaching out to the financial institution to seek resolution of the disputed data.

CIBIL does not charge for this service.

Things to note while raising a dispute
1. Complete the dispute request form
You can raise multiple dispute requests at a time.
2. Await the Bank/ financial institution's response
Rest assured we diligently follow up with the reporting banks/ financial institutions, however typical processing time may take upto 30 days.
3. Check your
dispute status
Login to your MyCIBIL portal to view the latest status of your Dispute.
Dispute Process lifecycle
Found an error or inaccuracies?
Incase an error is spotted
1
Fill & Submit the online dispute form
2
Marked as “Under Dispute”
Disputed section(s) and account(s) are marked "Under Dispute".
Investigation
Based on the type of dispute, the bank/ financial institution is identified.
CIBIL contacts the the concerned
bank/ financial institution.
Decision making
The bank/financial institute accepts or rejects the dispute.
If dispute is accepted
Corrections are made and user is notified.
If dispute is rejected
No corrections are made and user is notified.
Removed from “Under Dispute”
"Under Dispute" is removed from Disputed sections and accounts.
Turnaround time: Upto 30 days
