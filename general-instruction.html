General Instructions

You can initiate dispute(s) by following the steps provided above each section of your report.

  • You can dispute multiple accounts/enquiries/fields.

  • Please ensure to click on Submit Dispute once you have entered all of your dispute(s).

  • You will be able to raise disputes only for information reported by your Credit Institution(s).

  • Please contact your Credit Institution to correct any information that is not reported to TransUnion CIBIL.

  • You will see a confirmation screen mentioning whether the dispute was accepted or rejected along with either of the below reason for a rejection. The value entered is already updated in the report.

This information is not available with us. Please get in touch with the relevant Credit Institution(s) to submit the information to CIBIL. This information is already under dispute
