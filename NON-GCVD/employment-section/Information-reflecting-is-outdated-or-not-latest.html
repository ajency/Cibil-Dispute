Information reflecting is outdated / not latest eg: income, occupation

SOLUTION

Information available in this section is reported by your banks / financial institutions and is based on the data collected by them during your loan / credit card application.

This information will change once you update your latest information to your banks / credit institutions. In turn, they will report the updated information to TransUnion CIBIL, after which the details will be corrected here.

How much time it will take to raise this issue and get it resolved ?

Only once the change is authorized and provided by the concerned bank/financial institution, can your CIBIL Report be updated.

Please reach out to your bank / financial institution for any changes required.
